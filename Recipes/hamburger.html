Hamburger

Description

Hamburgers are one of the most popular dishes worldwide. It can be made in a ton of different
ways with a lot of different ingredients. It usually contains at least a hamburger patty and
some kind of bun around the patty. Desired vegatables or other additions can be added.

Ingredients

  • Burger patty
  • Hamburger buns
  • Bacon
  • Sliced cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Start by cooking your patty in a frying pan. Cook until it is close to well done
  2. While the patty is cooking, start cooking your bacon in another frying pan.
  3. The hamburger buns can be heated in the oven if desired, but this is not necessary.
  4. If you want any vegetables on your burger, start chopping them.
  5. When the hamburger is almost done, put a slice of cheese on top of it and let it melt for a bit
  6. Put all of your extra ingredients in between the burger and the top bun, and you're all good.
